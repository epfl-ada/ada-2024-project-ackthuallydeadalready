The Story Behind Wikipedia RFA

Requests for adminship (RFA) are pivotal in Wikipedia's governance. By analyzing votes and sentiments, we uncover fascinating patterns in community dynamics and decision-making processes.

Interactive Visualizations

Sentiment analysis reveals a spectrum of emotions influencing votes. Explore how positivity or negativity trends over time.

Key Findings

Trend Analysis

Neutral sentiments dominate voting, with notable spikes in positive language during successful campaigns.

Community Dynamics

Voting patterns reflect social clustering, with frequent voters often aligned on sentiment intensity.

Predictive Modeling

Our models suggest comment quality and frequency are strong predictors of electoral outcomes.
